I. CONCEPT

Construction

My exploration began with three core concepts: Experience, Network, and Information. By delving deeper into these interconnected ideas, the concept of "Cyborg" emerged as a critical point of intersection. To further examine this theme, I conducted a case study, analyzing the fictional character Makoto Kusanagi from the Ghost in the Shell franchise through the lens of human-technology integration. Finally, I translated my research findings into a visual and textual format, presenting them within a structured diagram.
